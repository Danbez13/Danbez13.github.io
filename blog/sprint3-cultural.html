Emotional intelligence

Emotional intelligence or EQ is a term created by two researchers Peter Savoy and John Mayer, and it is defined as the ability to recognize, understand and manage our emotions or the ability to recognize, understand and influence someone else's, it is the measure of the emotional intelligence a person has and shows the limits between the different feelings and their ability to use thinking to change their feelings and behaviour.

Your EQ is very different from IQ for example your IQ will secure you success in your academia whereas your EQ will ensure you success in life, unlike IQ which you are born with your EQ is a skill that can be improved upon through training and discipline.

Developing your EQ is a very important skill to have as it will help you find a job after finishing studies and is useful for everyday life especially when dealing with negative feelings and other people.
